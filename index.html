How it works

Talk to your agent. It does the rest.

01

Tell your agent what you want

Natural language. "Build me a conservative portfolio." "Go 20% into BTC when it hits fair value." No dashboards to click, no forms to fill.

02

Agent analyzes real market data

VWAP + standard deviation bands from live Kraken OHLC. Fear & greed index. Cross-venue funding rates. No opinions — just statistics.

03

Intents signed and routed through risk controls

Every trade intent signed with EIP-712, chain-bound with EIP-155. Submitted to a Risk Router contract that enforces limits before any execution happens.

04

Execution logged on-chain

Kraken CLI executes the trade. The outcome is recorded in an ERC-8004 Reputation Registry. Immutable. Auditable. No trust required.

05

You watch. Your dashboard updates.

Live portfolio view, P&L, trade history, and on-chain proof links. You see exactly what your agent did and why.

The Trust Stack

Verifiable by construction.

Agent Identity

Who is this agent? On-chain identity registry. Minted NFT. Auditable history.

EIP-712

Signed Intents

What is it trying to do? Every trade intent typed, structured, and cryptographically signed before execution.

EIP-155

Chain Binding

Locked to Base. Signed intents can't be replayed on other chains.

Risk Router

Position size, leverage, daily loss limits — enforced at the contract level. Not self-reported.
